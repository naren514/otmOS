Definitions - CLAIM_LINE_ITEM_COST

Stores costs on the claim line items

CLAIM_GID
Id for claim; along with LINE_ITEM_SEQ_NO, points to claim line item this cost is linked to
LINE_ITEM_SEQ_NO
Seq no of the claim line item this cost is tied to
COST_SEQ_NO
Sequence number to keep costs on claim lines unique
COST
Cost amount
COST_CURRENCY_GID
Currency for cost amount
COST_BASE
Base amount for cost
CLAIM_COST_TYPE_GID
Cost type such as DAMAGE or SALVAGE
