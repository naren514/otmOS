Definitions - INVOICE

One record per invoice. Main invoice table. Currently, an invoice relates to billing of one shipment, but in the future, multiple shipments may be consolidated onto an invoice. GC3 can populate this table from a shipment, or the invoice information can be fed into the system directly. An invoice is a relative term - a bill and an invoice look the same, but the person who receives it ("BILL TO" involved party) would consider it an invoice and the person generating it ("SERVICE PROVIDER" party) would consider it a bill.
