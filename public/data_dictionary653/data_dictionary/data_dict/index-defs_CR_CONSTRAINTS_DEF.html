Definitions - CR_CONSTRAINTS_DEF

Definition of various constraints for determining the feasibility of a CR Route.

MIN_TM_BTW_SHIPMTS
Minimum time between shipments of a route.
MAX_TM_BTW_SHIPMTS
Maximum time between shipments of a route.
MIN_DST_BTW_SHIPMTS
Minimum distance between shipments of a route.
MAX_DST_BTW_SHIPMTS
Maximum distance between shipments of a route.
MIN_DURATION
Minimum route duration.
MAX_DURATION
Maximum route duration.
MIN_DISTANCE
Minimum route distance.
MAX_DISTANCE
Maximum route distance.
MAX_DEADHEAD_DST
Maximum route deadhead distance.
MAX_DEADHEAD_PERCENT
Maximum percent of route distance that can be deadhead.
MAX_TM_BTW_RESTS
Maximum time between rests (for rough hours-of-service calculations).
MIN_REST_TIME
Minimum rest time (for rough hours-of-service calculations).
