Definitions - REMARK_QUAL_ASSET_ATTRIBUTE

Extends REMARK_QUAL table to define asset related attributes.

IS_FOR_DRIVER_CHK
Designate this special service is to be checked during the driver assignment.
IS_FOR_EQUIP_CHK
Designate this special service is to be checked during the equipment assignment.
IS_FOR_PU_CHK
Designate this special service is to be checked during the power unit assignment.
DRIVER_LEVEL
Designate this special service is to apply at the driver or driver type level.
EQUIP_LEVEL
Designate this special service is to apply at the equipment or equipment type level.
PU_LEVEL
Designate this special service is to apply at the power unit or power unit type level.
