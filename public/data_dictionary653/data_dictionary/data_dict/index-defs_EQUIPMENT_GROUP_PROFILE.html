Definitions - EQUIPMENT_GROUP_PROFILE

This table records the compatibility information between various equipment group profiles. For example a "Frozen" equipment group profile may not be compatible with "Heated" group profile.

EQUIPMENT_GROUP_PROFILE_GID
EQUIPMENT_GROUP_PROFILE_GID is the unique identifier for the equipment group profile.
EQUIPMENT_GROUP_PROFILE_XID
EQUIPMENT_GROUP_PROFILE_XID is the unique external identifier for the equipment group profile.
EQUIPMENT_GROUP_PROFILE_NAME
EQUIPMENT_GROUP_PROFILE_NAME is text for the name of the equipment group profile.
