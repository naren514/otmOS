Definitions - WORK_INVOICE

Includes a record of driver activities for a shipment. This is the record sent to payroll and used to calculate driver pay.

WORK_INVOICE_GID
Id for the work invoice
WORK_INVOICE_NUMBER
Id for the work invoice from a business standpoint; if a corrected work invoice is generated, this number will be retained
DRIVER_GID
Id of the driver for this work invoice
SECONDARY_DRIVER_GID
If driven by a team, the secondary driver id
DATE_SENT
Date this work invoice was sent to payroll
REVENUE
sell side revenue
