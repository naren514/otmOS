Definitions - CR_SCENARIO_SUMMARY

Solution summary information for a CR scenario.

NUM_LVFS
Number of lane volume forecasts.
TOTAL_VOLUME
Total volume on all LVFs for this project.
NUM_CRS
Number of CR Routes for this scenario.
CR_VOLUME
Total LVF volume covered by CR Routes for this scenario.
AVG_ROUTE_VOLUME
Average volume on CR routes.
NUM_LVFS_ON_CRS
Number of LVFs at least partially covered by CR routes for this scenario.
TOTAL_COST
Total cost of the current solution.
CR_COST
Total cost of the CR routes for the scenario.
SAVINGS
Savings for the scenario over market cost.
SAVINGS_PERCENT
Savings as a percent of total market cost.
NUM_CMT_CRS
Number of committed CR routes for this scenario.
CMT_CR_VOLUME
Total LVF volume covered by committed CR routes in this scenario.
AVG_CMT_ROUTE_VOLUME
Average volume on committed CR routes.
NUM_LVFS_ON_CMT_CRS
Number of lane volume forecasts at least partially covered by committed CR routes in this scenario.
TOTAL_CMT_COST
Total cost of the scenario considering only the CR routes that are committed and corresponding residual costs.
CMT_CR_COST
Total cost of the of the committed CR routes in the scenario.
CMT_SAVINGS
Total savings for the scenario considering only committed CR routes.
CMT_SAVINGS_PERCENT
Committed savings as a percent of total market cost.
