Definitions - CUSTOMER_SERVICE

This table keeps the customer services' info. And they will be used with assign customer services to order.

CUSTOMER_SERVICE_GID
Primary key of the table.
PRIORITY
Highest priority is 1. Bigger number has lower priority.
IS_ACTIVE
Only the active customer services will be assigned to order.
ORDER_RELEASE_TEMPLATE
OrderRelease with is_template='Y' and template_type='Service'
