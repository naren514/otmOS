Definitions - ITEM_SPECIAL_SERVICE

This table has a link to the special services table. It defines the special services required to ship or deliver an item. The special services differ from the accessories, in that the customer does not have to pay for the special services.

ITEM_GID
ITEM_GID contains the unique identifier for the item being shipped.
SPECIAL_SERVICE_GID
SPECIAL_SERVICE_GID contains the unique identifier for the special service required for the item being shipped.
