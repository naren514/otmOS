Definitions - COUNTRY_ZONE

Additional zone information for the country. Multiple countries can be part of a zone.

COUNTRY_ZONE_GID
COUNTRY_ZONE_GID contains the unique identifier for the country zone. Zones can include: AFR ANT ASI AUS CAM CAR EAF EUR FAR MID NAM NPL SAF SAM SAO
COUNTRY_ZONE_XID
COUNTRY_ZONE_XID contains the unique external identifier for the country zone.
