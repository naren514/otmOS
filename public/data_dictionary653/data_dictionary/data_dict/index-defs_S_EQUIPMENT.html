Definitions - S_EQUIPMENT

S_EQUIPMENT_GID
GC3 Global identifier for the entity. Is created by concatenating Domain Name,"." and XID.
S_EQUIPMENT_XID
A system generated unique identifier for the shipment equipment (without the domain).
EQUIPMENT_INITIAL
Number identifying the equipment used, which is not represented by a record in GC3's EQUIPMENT table (use EQUIPMENT_GID for that).
EQUIPMENT_NUMBER
Number identifying the equipment used, which is not represented by a record in GC3'S EQUIPMENT table (use EQUIPMENT_GID for that).
EQUIPMENT_TYPE_GID
GC3 Global identifier for the entity. Is created by concatenating Domain Name,"." and XID.
EQUIPMENT_GROUP_GID
GC3 Global identifier for the entity. Is created by concatenating Domain Name,"." and XID.
EQUIPMENT_GID
GC3 Global identifier for the entity. Is created by concatenating Domain Name,"." and XID.
WEIGHT_QUALIFIER
Indicates what the SCALE_WEIGHT and TARE_WEIGHT fields represent: Estimate, Net or Gross weight,
SCALE_WEIGHT
Weight recorded by scale when equipment is loaded.
SCALE_WEIGHT_UOM
Weight UOM for SCALE_WEIGHT
SCALE_WEIGHT_BASE
Weight base.
TARE_WEIGHT
Weight of equipment when empty.
TARE_WEIGHT_UOM
Weight UOM for TARE_WEIGHT
TARE_WEIGHT_BASE
Tare weight base.
SCALE_LOCATION
Placeholder for scale location information.
SCALE_NAME
Placeholder for scale name information.
SCALE_TICKET
Sequence ID printed on the scale ticket when weight is recorded.
INTERMODAL_EQUIP_LENGTH
Specially formatted field that is required by the railroads, representing the length of the equipement. It is in the format FtFtInIn, so 4803 would represent 48 feet, 3 inches.
SUBSTITUTE_EQUIPMENT_GROUP_GID
Equipment group substituted by carrier for this equipments
TYPE_ASSIGNMENT_CODE
Indicates the source of the EQUIPMENT_TYPE_GID
ASSIGNMENT_CODE
Indicates the source of the EQUIPMENT_GID
PICK_SKU_GID
With PICK_SKU_QUANTITY_TYPE_GID, this identifies the pickup inventory bucket adjusted during equipment type assignment.
DROP_SKU_GID
With DROP_SKU_QUANTITY_TYPE_GID, this identifies the dropoff inventory bucket adjusted during equipment type assignment.
FLOOR_HEIGHT
Actual height of the platform or floor above the ground or the rail.
EMPTY_CG_HEIGHT
When empty, indicates the height from Top of Rail to the Center of Gravity
DRY_CAPACITY
The maximum interior capacity of the equipment
LIQUID_CAPACITY
The number of gallons the equipment will hold
CALCULATED_GROSS_WT
The actual sum of the cargo plus the actual tare weight. A calculated version of a scale weight.
ACTUAL_LOAD_LIMIT
The maximum permissible weight of the commodity that can be loaded into the equipment.
ACTUAL_LENGTH
The inside length of the actual equipment from end to end inside walls, linings, and permanent bulkheads
ACTUAL_WIDTH
The inside width of the actual equipment from side walls and linings
ACTUAL_HEIGHT
The inside height of the actual equipment from the floor to the lowest point of the interior ceiling
