Definitions - CUTOFF_RECOVERY_TIME_D

Defination of Cutoff Recovery Time details. The records can specify cutoff or recovery, and can indicate a day-time or an offset (not both).

RATE_SERVICE_GID
Rate service.
SERVPROV_GID
Service provider.
LOCATION_GID
Location.
VOYAGE_SERVICE_TYPE_GID
Voyage Service Type.
OFFSET_TIME
Duration the delivery must arrive before service provider departs from origin or duration the delivery must wait after service provider arrives at destination.
OFFSET_TIME_UOM_CODE
Unit of measure for offset_time.
OFFSET_TIME_BASE
Comparison value.
DAY_OF_WEEK
Day of week.
HOUR_ADJUSTMENT
Hour adjustment from midnight.
MINUTE_ADJUSTMENT
Minute Adjustment.
IS_CUTOFF
Indicates the record is defined for cutoff or recovery time. When 'Y', it's for cutoff time; when 'N', it's for recovery time.
IS_OFFSET
When 'Y', cutoff/recovery time is defined as offset; when 'N', cutoff/recovery time is defined as day-time.
CUTOFF_TYPE
Cutoff type. It can have one of the following values: CY: "Container Yard" - CY cutoff is the gate house time to arrive at the ocean carriers facility. SI "Shipping Instruction" - SI cutoff is the required time for shippers information to be provided to the forwarder. CFS: "Container Freight Station" - CFS Cutoff is the required time to be at a 3rd party location for container packing.
