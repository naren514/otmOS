Definitions - CR_LANE_COMMITMENT

Scenario-specific commitment data for lane volume forecasts.

TOTAL_VOLUME
Total volume for the LV Forecast.
MARKET_COST
Per-unit market cost for the LV forecast.
CR_VOLUME_PERCENT
CR volume as a percent of total volume
CMT_CR_VOLUME_PERCENT
Committed CR volume as a percent of total volume.
CR_VOLUME
LVF volume covered by CR routes for this scenario.
CR_CONFIDENCE_FACTOR
Forecast confidence factor at the CR volume.
CR_COST
Average per-unit volume allocated cost for this LV forecast on CR routes for this scenario.
CR_SAVINGS_PERCENT
CR Leg savings as percent of market cost.
CMT_CR_VOLUME
LVF volume covered by committed CR routes for this scenario.
CMT_CR_CONFIDENCE_FACTOR
Forecast confidence factor at the committed CR volume.
CMT_CR_COST
Average per-unit volume allocated cost for this LV forecast on committed CR routes for this scenario.
CMT_CR_SAVINGS_PERCENT
Committed CR Leg savings as percent of market cost.
