Definitions - EQUIPMENT

This table acts as the equipment master for the system. It stores the information about all the equipments.

EQUIPMENT_GID
EQUIPMENT_GID is the unique identifier for equipment.
EQUIPMENT_XID
EQUIPMENT_TYPE_XID is the unique external identifier for the equipment type.
EQUIPMENT_NAME
EQUIPMENT_NAME is text to describe the type of equipment. Examples include: CAR LOADS, TANKER, TRUCKS
EQUIPMENT_TYPE_GID
EQUIPMENT_TYPE_GID is the unique identifier for the equipment type.
OWNER_NAME
OWNER_NAME contains text for the name of the equipment owner.
EQUIPMENT_OWNER_TYPE
The Type of Owner for this Equipment. C = Carrier Owned, L = Lessee, P = Private, A = All
LESSEE_GID
The SCAC for the Lessee of the Equipment.
TARE_WEIGHT
The empty weight of the Equipment by itself.
AAR_CAR_TYPE
The Association of American Railroads Car Type.
DATE_BUILT
The date this equipment was built
PURCH_DATE
The purchase date of this equipment
REGISTRATION_NUM
Registration number
REGISTRATION_DATE
The date of the registration
LICENSE_PLATE
The license plate number
LICENSE_STATE
The license state
SIGHTING_LOCATION_GID
Sighting location of the equipment
SIGHTING_DATE
Time of the sighting.
PREV_SIGHTING_LOCATION_GID
Previous sighting location of the equipment
PREV_SIGHTING_DATE
Time of the previous sighting
INTERCHANGE_RECV_LOCATION_GID
Interchange receive location of the equipment
INTERCHANGE_RECV_DATE
Time of the interchange receive
IS_CONTAINER
Yes/No indicator for whether the equipment is a container
CURRENT_CHASSIS_INITIAL
Initial of the current chassis
CURRENT_CHASSIS_NUMBER
Number of the current chassis
LAST_OUTGATE_LOCATION_GID
The location where the equipment (chassis/container or trailer) was last outgated
LAST_OUTGATE_TIME
Outgate time stamp at the outbound ramp
LAST_INGATE_TIME
Ingate time stamp at the inbound ramp
PARK_LOCATION_GID
Park location of the equipment
UM_CHARGE
UMLER Mileage Charge
UM_CHARGE_CURR_GID
UMLER Mileage Charge Currency
UM_CHARGE_BASE
UMLER Mileage Charge Base Amount
UM_CHARGE_UNIT
UMLER Mileage Charge Unit (example, 2)
UM_CHARGE_UNIT_BASE
UMLER Mileage Charge Unit in Base UOM
UM_CHARGE_UNIT_UOM_CODE
UMLER Mileage Charge Unit (example, Mile)
AAR_MECH_DESIG
AAR Mechanical Designation
EQUIP_MAX_GROSS_WT
The maximum permissible weight of the equipment and the load.
FLOOR_HEIGHT
Actual height of the platform or floor above the ground or the rail.
EMPTY_CG_HEIGHT
When empty, indicates the height from Top of Rail to the Center of Gravity
DRY_CAPACITY
The maximum interior capacity of the equipment
LIQUID_CAPACITY
The number of gallons the equipment will hold
CAR_DESTINATION_LOCATION_GID
Specifies destination for the car
CAR_DESTINATION_ETA_DATE
This is the estimated arrival time at the car destination
