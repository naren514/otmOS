Definitions - INVOICE_EQUIPMENT_SEAL

The imprinted serial number of a "one use" locking device the shipper places to prohibit access to the cargo. The consignee must destroy the device to remove it.

INVOICE_GID
INVOICE_GID is the unique identifier for the invoice.
SEAL_SEQUENCE
Seal sequence number.
SEAL_NUMBER
SEAL_NUMBER contains the identification number for equipment that has been sealed.
