DEF_COST_ABOVE
The default cost an invoice may be above the shipment cost for auto approve. The lesser of this amount, if specified, and the amount calculated by the default cost percent above will be used.
DEF_COST_BELOW
The default cost an invoice may be below the shipment cost for auto approve. The lesser of this amount, if specified, and the amount calculated by the default cost percent below will be used.
