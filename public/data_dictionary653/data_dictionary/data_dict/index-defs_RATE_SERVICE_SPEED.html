Definitions - RATE_SERVICE_SPEED

This table is used to estimate the time of delivery. It stores estimated average speed for ranges of distances, and all includes minimum times. It assumes that the farther the distance, the faster the average speed will be. Ex: A truck on average might drive 10 miles/hr for a distance of 20 miles, because of the amount of time it takes to drive out of the town, get on the higway, and then immediately slow down to deliver the goods to the next town. However, if the truck was taking the goods across 500 miles, then the average speed might be 40 miles an hour, since the truck is on the highway much longer. Based upon a lookup in this table, a rough estimate of delivery can be made.
