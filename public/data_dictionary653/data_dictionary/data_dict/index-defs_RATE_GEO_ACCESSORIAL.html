Definitions - RATE_GEO_ACCESSORIAL

Anticipated additional costing due to requirements of the type of shipment for that particular travel path (lane). For example, a carrier may charge extra for handling large loads on trucks into New York City, while another may not. By anticipating these additional costs, the GC3 engine can better determine which carrier is really going to be the most economical for a specific shipment.
