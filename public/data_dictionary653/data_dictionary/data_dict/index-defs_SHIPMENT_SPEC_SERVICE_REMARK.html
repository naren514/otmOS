Definitions - SHIPMENT_SPEC_SERVICE_REMARK

Allows you to define remarks for why this special services applies to this shipment .

SHIPMENT_GID
The shipment that this remark is associated with.
SHIPMENT_SPECIAL_SERVICE_SEQ
sequence for the shipment special service
REMARK_SEQUENCE
The one-up number to make the remark unique.
REMARK_QUAL_GID
The remark qualifier, or type.
REMARK_TEXT
The remark text itself.
