Definitions - INVOICE_SUMMARY_DETAIL

INVOICE_SUMMARY_DETAIL contains accessorial charges that may relate to an invoice as a whole, rather than a specific line item. Examples can include a bunker surcharge or Panama Canal charge. The table also may contain freight charges as a line item.
