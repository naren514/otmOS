Definitions - SERVPROV_POLICY

Maintain service provider insurance policy information.

SERVPROV_POLICY_SEQNO
Unique number
TYPE
Defines the type of policy
EFFECTIVE_DATE
Date the policy becomes effective
EXPIRATION_DATE
Date the policy expires
POLICY_NUMBER
Policy number for the policy
ISSUER
The issuer of the policy
COVERAGE_AMOUNT
The coverage amount for the policy
COVERAGE_AMOUNT_CURRENCY_GID
The currency associated with coverage amount
COVERAGE_AMOUNT_BASE
The base version of the coverage amount
SERVPROV_GID
The service provider for which this policy applies
