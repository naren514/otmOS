Definitions - EQUIP_AXLE_LOADING_WEIGHT

AXLE_SEQUENCE_NO
Axle sequence number.
AXLE_NAME
User specified name for the axle where the distance and max weight are defined, such as front axle or real axle.
AXLE_DISTANCE
The distance from the nose of the equipment where the max weight can be loaded.
MAX_WEIGHT
The max loading weight allowed.
