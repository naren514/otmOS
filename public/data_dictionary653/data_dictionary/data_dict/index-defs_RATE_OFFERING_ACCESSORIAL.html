Definitions - RATE_OFFERING_ACCESSORIAL

RATE_OFFERING_ACCESSORIAL contains the anticipated additional cost due to requirements of the type of shipment. For example, one carrier may charge extra for Kosher shipments while another carrier already includes such charges in its rates. By anticipating these additional costs, the GC3 engine can better determine which carrier is really going to be the most economical for a specific shipment.
