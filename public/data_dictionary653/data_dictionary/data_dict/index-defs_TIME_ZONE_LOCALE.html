Definitions - TIME_ZONE_LOCALE

This table is used to define the environment of the time zone, including the countries, states and cities in various time zones across the countries. This is used to schedule shipments and also to achieve full globalization in the GC3.

TIME_ZONE_GID
TIME_ZONE_GID contains the unique identifier for the time zone for localization.
COUNTRY_CODE3_GID
GC3 Global identifier for the entity. Is created by concatenating Domain Name,"." and XID.
CITY
CITY contains the name of the city for the time zone.
