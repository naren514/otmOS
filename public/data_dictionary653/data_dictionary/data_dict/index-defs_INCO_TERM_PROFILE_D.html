Definitions - INCO_TERM_PROFILE_D

The INCO_TERM_PROFILE_D table maps INCO terms to the INCO term profiles.

INCO_TERM_PROFILE_GID
The INCO term profile.
INCO_TERM_GID
The INCO term.
INCO_TERM_PROFILE_D_SEQNO
Sequence number.
TERM_LOCATION_TEXT
Optional field to include the term location text with the INCO term in the profile.
