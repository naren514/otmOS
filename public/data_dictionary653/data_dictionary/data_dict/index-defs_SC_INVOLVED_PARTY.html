Definitions - SC_INVOLVED_PARTY

Involved parties to be added to secondary charge shipment upon creation

SECONDARY_CHARGE_RULE_GID
The secondary charge rule this involved party is associated with
INVOLVED_PARTY_QUAL_GID
A pointer to the unique idenifier for the involved party. The involved party qual can be: BILL-FROM BILL-TO CONSIGNEE CUSTOMER-NUMBER LOGISTICS ORDEROWNER ORDERSUPPLIER REMIT-TO SHIPPER etc.
INVOLVED_PARTY_CONTACT_GID
Contact ID for this involved party
COM_METHOD_GID
Communications method for this involved party, e.g. EMAIL, FAX
