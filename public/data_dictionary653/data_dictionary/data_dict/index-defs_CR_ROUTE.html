Definitions - CR_ROUTE

Cooperative Route.

VOLUME
Volume in number of shipments per leg.
ORIGINAL_VOLUME
Volume assigned by CR Solver
UNIT_COST
Per unit-volume cost.
UNIT_SAVINGS
Per unit-volume savings.
SAVINGS_PERCENT
% savings over market cost.
CONFIDENCE_FACTOR
Confidence factor (%) based upon constituent forecast leg confidence factors and scenario volumes.
COMMITTED_CONFIDENCE_FACTOR
Confidence factor (%) based upon constituent forecast leg confidence factors and scenario commitments.
DURATION
Duration of the CR Route.
IS_FEASIBLE
Indicates feasibility based upon scenario and fleet constraints.
IS_COMMITTED
Indicates whether the CR route has been committed by the user.
IS_USER_EDITED
Indicates whether the CR route has been edited by the user.
IS_UNPROCESSED
Indicates whether the CR route has been processed to determine distances and costs.
