Definitions - EQUIPMENT_MARK

Equipment Marks are a sequence of two to four letters that uniquely identifies the owner of a piece of equipment.

EQUIPMENT_MARK_GID
The unique sequence of two to four letters representing the Equipment Mark for a piece of Equipment.
EQUIPMENT_MARK_XID
The sequence of two to four letters representing the Equipment Mark for a piece of Equipment.
OWNER_NAME
The Owner Name of the Equipment Mark.
DESCRIPTION
The Description of the Equipment Mark.
