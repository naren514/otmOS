Definitions - RATE_GEO_COST_WEIGHT_BREAK

Indicates the load level in which the carrier starts to offer a discount. For example, 1000 lbs of coal might be charged as .25/lb but be .15/lb once the carrier is asked to ship 10,000 lbs.
