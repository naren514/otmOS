Definitions - RATE_GEO

Also known as a lane, a rate geo is a prescribed course for ships, aircraft, etc. that can have costing (or time of travel) associated with it. A rate geo may be something as general as West Coast to East Coast, country to country, state to state, or as specific as zip code to zip code. GC3 will always look at the most granular rate_geo it can find.
